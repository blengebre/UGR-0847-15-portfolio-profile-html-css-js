I went to Hawassa 3 years ago. It was so beautiful and clean. Location: Situated about 275 kilometers (171 miles) south of Addis Ababa, Ethiopia's capital, along the Trans-African Highway. Lake Hawassa: The city lies on the shores of Lake Hawassa, a freshwater lake famous for its tranquil beauty and rich biodiversity.

I went to Bahir Dar 2 years ago and visited Lake Tana. Lake Tana is the largest lake in Ethiopia and a prominent natural feature in Africa. Location: Lake Tana is situated in the northwestern Ethiopian Highlands in the Amhara region, near the city of Bahir Dar. It covers an area of approximately 3,673 square kilometers (1,418 square miles).
