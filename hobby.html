Below are some key details about Lake Tana: Location: Lake Tana is situated in the northwestern Ethiopian Highlands in the Amhara region, near the city of Bahir Dar. Size: It covers an area of approximately 3,673 square kilometers (1,418 square miles) and has an average depth of around 9 meters (30 feet), with its maximum depth reaching about 14 meters (46 feet). Source of the Blue Nile: Lake Tana is the main source of the Blue Nile River, which flows out of the lake and joins the White Nile in Sudan to form the Nile River.
